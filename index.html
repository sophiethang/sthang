About The Restaurant

The Restaurant was founded by a group of friends who started out being avid foodies
and would often go to places far and wide to taste-test the best of the best.

From this passion of theirs, they decided to pursue a career in the food industry where
they would be able to create an atmosphere and menu that anyone can enjoy.
